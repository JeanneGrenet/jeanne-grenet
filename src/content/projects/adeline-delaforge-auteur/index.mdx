## Overview

A showcase website for **Adeline Delaforge**, a French author, designed to showcase her work and grow her audience.

## Website architecture

### Hero
Full-width banner with the author's name, the navigation. Links to different social media and external selling website.

### Books
Grid of book cards, each displaying the cover, title, synopsis and purchase links (Amazon, Fnac, etc.).

### About
A short biography section with a photo and a few paragraphs about the author's background and writing journey.

### Blog / News
A feed of the latest articles, events or announcements (book signings, new releases, interviews).

### Social & Contact
Links to social media profiles (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) and a simple contact form for collaboration requests.

*The website is still in development for now but visual insights will be available later.*
